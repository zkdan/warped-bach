GETTING HERE

  • Regardless of where you're coming from, you will likely end up on the Gardiner Expressway. The closest exit to the hotel is exit 154 onto York St.

    There are four 24-hour, paid parking lots within a few hundred metres of the hotel if you're driving. There is one (1) free parking space at Charlotte's house available first come first served if you want to carpool!

  • If you're arriving by Go Transit, you'll be able to Uber or TTC from Union Station.

    Friday or Weekend Pass holders:

    If you can arrive before 4 p.m. on Friday and would like a ride from Union Station to the hotel, please let Charlotte know and that can be arranged!

    Saturday Pass holders:

    If you can arrive before 10 a.m. on Saturday and would like a ride from Union Station to the hotel, please let Charlotte know and that can be arranged!

  • To the hotel:

    From Union Station:

    Take the 1 line (Yonge-University) towards Vaughan Metropolitan Centre Station to Osgoode Station

    Walk 1 small block south on University Ave to Richmond St W

    You'll see The Hilton on the corner!

    From within Toronto:

    The closest subway stop is Osgoode Station

    The closest streetcar stop is the 501 Queen stop at Queen and University
